Some of My Hobbies

Hello fellow internet readers, my name is Robert and I would like to tell you about some of the hobbies that I have.

Lets start with gaming as my first hobbie. It started when I was a little kid that my first games I played was "Mario Bros". I am sure that a lot of you may have started or remembered this game, this was were it all started. From there on I have gone threw a lot of games and consoles, one that has always stuck was "Golden Eye" for the N64. Now we have gone threw many systems and many more games, we are in a time were they are getting more realistic and it is a game to see which game will last the longest before we turn to the next one.

With this said, I have been playing a game called "Rainbow Six Siege" this game has been one that I have played for a year now. The reason I have been so hooked on the game is because it is a first person shooter that you need tactics and communication with your team/players to succed. Granted this game may be a lot of kill the other team and do it again, but the tactical aspect makes it great.

Another hobby of mine is Photography, this has been a recently new thing for me. I started it like 2 years ago and it was not until last year that I got more into it and improving on my pictures. I love taking landscape shots mostly during the night or sunset, the reason I have chosen to do night photography is because there are a lot of people that don't know how beautiful the world is at night. I will show some of my work in this blog, I really hope you like the shots. If you would like to see the gameplay click on the link and see for yourself Rainbow Six Siege.

I hope you have enjoyed the little blog of mine, I will be adding more of my hobbies later on.
